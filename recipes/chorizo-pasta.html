Chorizo Pasta

The best pasta during a cold winter!

Chorizo Pasta will warm you up during winter evenings. You can spice them up as you like!

Ingredients list

  • Fusillis pasta
  • Chorizo
  • Vegetal oil
  • Chorizo
  • onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbes de provence
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Fusillis pasta in boiling water during 10 minutes
  2. Cut onions and chorizo
  3. Add Tomatoes to onions and chorizo
  4. Cook over low heat the chorizo sauce
  5. Add chorizo sauce to pasta
  6. Enjoy!
