Raclette

How to make a good French Raclette?

Raclette is more than just a meal. It's a party where everyone at table is participating. There is something for everyone. You don't like vegetable ? Cold cuts will be be your best friends. With Raclette you can improvise ! It's a party where cheese is the main ingredient.

Ingredients list

  • Potatoes
  • Raclette cheese
  • Vegetables of your choice (mushrooms, onions, salad ...)
  • Cold cuts: ham, rosette, salami (everything you like)

Equipment

  • Raclette machine

Steps

  1. Cook the potatoes
    • Steaming during 20 minutes
    • In boilling water during 40 minutes
  2. Put the dish with cooked potatoes on the Raclette Machine
  3. Put the spatula fill with cheese in the Raclette machine
  4. Enjoy by spreading the melted cheese on your potatoes!
