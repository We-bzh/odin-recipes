Gratin Dauphinois

And potatoes again , with this South-est French meal!

The Gratin Dauphinois is made of potatoes and sour cream. It's a sweet meal which will bring you comfort.

Ingredients list

  • Potatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Cook the potatoes
    • Steaming during 20 minutes
    • In boilling water during 40 minutes
  2. Peel and cut potatoes into slices
  3. Chop the garlic
  4. Boil the milk, garlic, pepper and salt with potatoes during 15 minutes
